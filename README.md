# State my Values by Thinking About my Ethical Nightmares
My company/my concentration, is cybersecurity, so my ethical nightmare is allowing in mistakes that could have been avoided. These mistakes can end up hurting people's livelihoods, like if the mistake is around money or personal information. So my values would be around taking as many painstaking preventative measures as possible. Then another value I have is around responsibility. Specifically that if anything were to go awry after I already have preventative measures in place. If this were to happen, I will have already set up more rules to stop the security issue from continuing or spreading. Thus, I know my responsibility to the poeple and the great amount of faith they've put in me. 



# Explain Why I Value What I Do in a Way that Connects to my Organization's Mission or Purpose
I am a security professional that keeps safe people's entire livelihoods, everything they work for each day. Even a slight breach in security can cause someone to loose rent money or money for food for their families. Our clients give us control over the part of their lives that they can't control. How their personal information is shared illegally on the internet. Even if a user were to act in a responsible manner, info can still be shared through breaches in reputable sites. Thus having as many security measures in place as possible, and always finding new preventatives, is my purpose. I will not let even a little breach go, because one opening could become a tunnel for bad actors to take. Then thousands of people could be at risk. Thus, prevatatives and responsibility of setting up many measures in place are needed.



# Connect my Values to What I take to be Ethically Impermissible 
Preventatives and responsibility. If I am on a break after a work day and I get a breach alert, I will never just wait until the next day, or let someone less experienced take care of it. I will spend all my time and resources to ensure safety. 


# Articulate How I Will Realize My Ethical Goals Or Avoid My Ethical Nightmares
Authenticity. We will already have procedures for our customers code. I will takes measures to check around the clock for issues. Like with automated functions such as Amazon Guardduty or Amazon AWS. I will make sure each company has their own personalized checks for their code. These checks will test for any known breaches that could happen, and also if there are any signs of a potential bad actors having gotten into the code. Authenticity comes in here because if my initial checks allow a bad actor to get in, I will report to the customer/user, and find as many ways as possible to quickly fix what has happened.
